🌟 About Iqsa

Iqsa is a dedicated guidance website created to support BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) students by providing high-quality notes, useful projects, and exam preparation materials. This platform is especially designed for students who want to succeed academically and professionally.

👨‍💻 About the Creator

I’m Mohammed Sufiyan, a BCA student and passionate developer. I created Iqsa as a free and helpful platform to assist other BCA students like myself with easy access to study materials and project ideas.

I’m also a digital invitation designer and enjoy building useful tech tools for students and friends. You can find me on Instagram at:
@sufiiyn

🎯 Our Goal

  • 📌 Make learning easier for BCA students
  • 📌 Provide free handwritten and digital notes
  • 📌 Inspire students to build real-world projects
  • 📌 Support students in their academic journey
